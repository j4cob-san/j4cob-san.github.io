Our Story

Jasmine and Jacob first met in 2012 during a cinema trip organised by a mutual friend, and there was an instant connection. Since then, they’ve spent the last 14 years adventuring through life together, sharing a love for rock climbing, travelling, and dancing together at festivals.


During an unforgettable trip around America, Jacob proposed to Jasmine in San Francisco’s beautiful Golden Gate Park, beside the vibrant dahlias. It was the perfect moment in a journey they’ve been building together ever since.


Now they can’t wait to celebrate the next chapter of their adventure with the people they love most.
